I've always interested in simple geometry forms like triangles, rectangles, or circles. To create my own typeface, the most important visual criteria was to make them fit into a geometry shape. Also, I wanted it to consist of simple figures. Some ideation sketches are below.

The first idea was a set of typeface on one grid lines. Also, I tried to give some visual aspects to make it more interesting. For example, I tried to give them different line weight or colors but in a consistent concept so that they can be recognized as one type of typeface.

After the first typeface ideation, I wanted something that has more geometric concept. What draw my interest was triangle with a long slope. So I tried another sketch that meets my personal taste.

Then I put some decoration and make the lines weights different. Instead of practical use or readability, I focused more on the triangular shape. After all, I finally come to make the final typeface.
